Audio CODECs convert analog signals to digital and vice versa, ensuring high-quality audio transmission. Audio DSPs (Digital Signal Processors) enhance sound quality through various algorithms and effects. Audio SoCs (System on Chips) integrate multiple functions into a single chip, facilitating compact designs in devices like smartphones and smart speakers, thereby driving innovation in audio technology.</p></p>
