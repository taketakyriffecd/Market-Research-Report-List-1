<p><strong>The Automotive Vacuum Brake Booster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Camshaft-driven Vacuum Brake Booster</li><li>Engine-driven Vacuum Brake Booster</li><li>Electric Vacuum Brake Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive vacuum brake booster market is segmented into three main types: camshaft-driven, engine-driven, and electric vacuum brake boosters. Camshaft-driven boosters utilize engine camshaft movement to generate vacuum, enhancing brake force. Engine-driven boosters rely on a direct connection to the engine’s intake manifold for vacuum supply. In contrast, electric vacuum boosters operate independently of the engine, using an electric pump to create vacuum pressure. Each type offers distinct advantages in performance and application within various vehicle systems.</p></p>
