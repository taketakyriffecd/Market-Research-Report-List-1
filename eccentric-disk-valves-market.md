<p><p>Eccentric disk valves are designed for precise flow control and come primarily in two types: wafer and lug. Wafer type valves are compact and installed between flanges, providing a lightweight solution often used in space-constrained applications. In contrast, lug type valves have protruding lugs for direct attachment, allowing for easier maintenance and installation without needing to disconnect the entire piping system. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, ensuring efficient and reliable operation in fluid management systems.</p></p>
