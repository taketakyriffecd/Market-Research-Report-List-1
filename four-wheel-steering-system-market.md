<p><p>The four-wheel steering system market includes several types, each enhancing vehicle maneuverability. Crab steering allows all wheels to turn in the same direction, improving lateral movement, ideal for tight spaces. Passive rear-wheel steering enables the rear wheels to turn slightly in the opposite direction at lower speeds for enhanced stability. Articulated steering involves a hinge-like connection between vehicle sections, allowing for better navigation in challenging terrains. Together, these systems enhance handling, stability, and overall driving performance.</p></p>
