<p><p>The grease fittings market can be categorized into three main types: straight, 45-degree, and 90-degree fittings. Straight fittings allow for easy access to lubrication points directly, while the 45-degree fittings provide a slight angle for better accessibility in tight spaces. The 90-degree fittings are designed to accommodate challenging angles, facilitating lubrication in hard-to-reach areas. Each type is essential for proper maintenance of machinery and equipment, ensuring optimal performance and longevity by allowing effective grease application at various orientations.</p></p>
