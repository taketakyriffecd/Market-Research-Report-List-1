<p><p>The passenger vehicle parking sensor market is primarily divided into two types: forward and rear view sensors. Forward sensors are installed at the front of the vehicle, helping drivers detect obstacles while maneuvering into tight spaces or during forward parking. Rear view sensors, on the other hand, are positioned at the back, assisting in detecting objects when reversing. Both systems enhance safety and convenience, reducing the likelihood of collisions and improving parking accuracy in various driving scenarios.</p></p>
