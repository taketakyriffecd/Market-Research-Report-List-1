<p><p>The Polyethylene Oxide (PEO) film market comprises three main types: Instant Film, Medium Soluble Membrane, and Hard to Crystallize Film. Instant Film is designed for quick dissolution, ideal for applications needing rapid action, like drug delivery. Medium Soluble Membrane offers a balanced release rate, suitable for sustained applications. Hard to Crystallize Film features enhanced stability and durability, making it suitable for more demanding environments. Each type serves distinct applications within industries such as pharmaceuticals, packaging, and biotechnology.</p></p>
