<p><p>The Regional Anesthesia Disposables Market is categorized into three main types: spinal, epidural, and peripheral. Spinal anesthesia involves injecting anesthetic into the spinal canal to block sensation in lower body areas, commonly used for surgeries like cesarean sections. Epidural anesthesia targets the epidural space to provide pain relief during labor and surgical procedures. Peripheral anesthesia blocks sensation in specific limbs or regions, enhancing pain management in procedures such as orthopedic surgeries. Each type utilizes specialized disposable equipment for safety and hygiene.</p></p>
