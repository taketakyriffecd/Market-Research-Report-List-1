<p><p>Single Selective Pallet Racking consists of two primary market types: Roll Form Type and Structural Steel Type. Roll Form Type uses cold-formed steel, offering flexibility and ease of installation, making it suitable for lighter loads and less demanding environments. In contrast, Structural Steel Type employs heavy-duty welded beams and uprights, providing superior strength and durability, ideal for high-capacity storage and heavier loads in industrial settings. Both types enhance warehouse efficiency by allowing quick access to individual pallets.</p></p>
